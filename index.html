The Free Gift

SALVATION! What an amazing gift from God: "the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord. Romans 6:23b"
There is forgiveness through the blood of Jesus.” It is not a reward for our services; it is the gift of God through Jesus Christ.
It is not given because we are worthy to receive it, but because it is a gift worthy of God to give. John 3:16.
The gospel of salvation is just this:
A simple offer on God’s side and simple acceptance on ours!
A gift we do not deserve as a wretched sinner, but made qualified to possess once we have confessed Jesus Christ as our Lord and personal saviour.
The reason Jesus came to this world- His birth we are celebrating today!
He- Jesus Christ was crucified so that “whoever believes in Him should not perish but have everlasting life” (John 3:16)-paid our debt
Rose up on the third day and back to heaven to prepare a place for us. 1 Corinthians 2:9
You are urged to accept this precious gift. Do not hesitate; only believe! Matthew 6:33
These are the results of salvation in Christ.
Your first step is confident acceptance of an offer that is free, generous, and unconditional.
It is completely free!
What can be more sure or more free than this?
Salvation without money, Salvation without price,
Salvation for the guilty, Salvation for the lost;
Salvation for the wretched, The sad and tempest-tossed.
Our sins were borne by Jesus, The Substitute from God;
He took them all, and freed us from sin’s accursed load.
Our guilt was borne by Jesus, Who washed the crimson stains White in His blood most precious,
Until no spot remains.

Accept Jesus Christ Today!
